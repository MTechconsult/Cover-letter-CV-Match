How the generator works

Eminent Media’s script tokenizes the job description, removes common stopwords, counts frequency of remaining words and selects the top phrases to reference in the cover letter. It blends your CV bullets and the role's key responsibilities into a concise 3-paragraph letter suitable for pasting as an email body or as an attached cover letter.

Suggested method of application (simple & effective)

  1. Customize: Tailor the generated letter with at least one specific achievement that matches a job requirement.
  2. Subject line: Use a clear subject like "Application: [Job Title] — [Your Name]".
  3. Attachment naming: Save your CV as "Firstname-Lastname-CV.pdf" and cover letter as "Firstname-Lastname-CoverLetter.pdf".
  4. Email body: Paste the tailored letter as the email body. Keep attachments minimal — PDF CV, optionally a one-page cover letter.
  5. Follow-up: If no reply after 7–10 days, send a polite follow-up referencing the role and date you applied.
  6. Apply widely but smartly: Prioritize roles where your top skills match the JD keywords.

Quick checklist before sending

  • Spelling & grammar checked
  • Contact details correct
  • CV highlights aligned with the job
  • Files named professionally
